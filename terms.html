2. PAYMENT

  • Payment is due on completion of service unless otherwise agreed (e.g., property managers on NET 30 terms).

4. WHAT WE WILL DO

5. WHAT WE WON'T DO

  • Move hazardous materials: paint, gas, propane, oil, asbestos, chemicals, ammunition

  • Move illegal items
  • Work in unsafe conditions (severe weather, structural hazards)

6. INSURANCE & LIABILITY

We carry $2 million general liability insurance.

OUR DAMAGE-FREE GUARANTEE: In 500+ jobs we have zero damage claims. But if we ever damage one of your items or your property, we will repair or replace it within reasonable depreciated value. We do not cover items packed by the customer that show no external damage (boxes are packed at your risk).

Maximum liability is limited to:

  • $10,000 per job for residential moves
  • Replacement cost for items under $1,000
  • Depreciated value for older items over $1,000
  • We are NOT liable for: pre-existing damage, items not declared in advance (e.g. antiques worth over $5,000 not disclosed), or "acts of God" (severe weather, accidents outside our control)

7. ITEMS LEFT BEHIND

For junk removal: once you tell us to take something, it's gone — we cannot retrieve items from the landfill. Double-check before pointing.

We may take photos of work in progress (before/after of empty rooms or stuff in our truck, no identifying customer info or faces without consent) to share on our website/social media. If you prefer NOT to be photographed, just tell us — no problem.
