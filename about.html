🔥 WHY "FIRE FELLAS"?

Because Calgary deserved a moving and junk crew with fire — fast, hot, unstoppable — and fellas — friendly locals who do the work ourselves.

Not a franchise. Not a national chain with a call centre in Toronto. Not a guy with a Kijiji ad and a borrowed pickup. We're actual Calgary fellas who built this from the ground up.

"We treat your stuff like our own. Every job. Every time. Or we don't take it on at all."

📅 HOW IT STARTED

The idea was simple: Calgarians were tired of sloppy movers, sketchy haulers, and surprise invoices.

We watched friends get burned. Grandparents pressured into overpaying. Reno crews leaving messes that no one would haul. People scared to hire random guys off classified ads because of horror stories.

So we lit a fire under the whole thing. One truck. Two fellas. A handful of jobs. Word spread. We added a second truck. Then a third crew. Today we serve hundreds of Calgary families a year — and the model hasn't changed.
