5📥 LEAD MAGNET — "Calgary Moving Day Checklist"

Free PDF you give away in your Instagram bio: "DM us 'CHECKLIST' for our free Calgary Moving Day Checklist!" → captures contact + builds trust BEFORE they need a mover.

YOUR CALGARY
MOVING DAY CHECKLIST 🔥

BROUGHT TO YOU BY FIRE FELLAS CALGARY

📅 4 WEEKS BEFORE YOUR MOVE

  • Get 3 quotes from local Calgary movers (call us at (368) 299-7792 for yours!)
  • Declutter — donate / sell what you don't need (less = cheaper move)
  • Order packing supplies (boxes, tape, bubble wrap, sharpies)
  • Notify your landlord / Calgary utilities of move date
  • Book moving day off work

📅 2 WEEKS BEFORE

  • Pack non-essentials (off-season clothes, books, decor)
  • Update address: bank, ENMAX, Direct Energy, City of Calgary, doctors
  • Confirm moving day with movers (1-week reminder text!)
  • Forward mail through Canada Post
  • Plan transport for kids / pets on moving day

📅 1 WEEK BEFORE

  • Pack a "first-night" box: toilet paper, snacks, charger, sheets, kettle
  • Empty & defrost your fridge / freezer
  • Confirm parking at both addresses (especially for downtown / condos)
  • Withdraw cash for tipping movers ($20-50 per mover is standard in Calgary)
  • Take photos of any valuables BEFORE the move (insurance)

📅 MOVING DAY

  • Be on-site 30 min before movers arrive
  • Walk-through with movers: show fragile, heavy, "load last" items
  • Keep important documents + valuables WITH YOU (not on truck)
  • Final walk-through of old place before leaving (check every closet!)
  • Tip your movers if they crushed it 🔥

📅 FIRST WEEK IN NEW PLACE

  • Unpack ESSENTIALS box first (kitchen, bathroom, bedroom)
  • Test all utilities: water, gas, internet, heat
  • Find your nearest grocery, pharmacy, gas station
  • Meet your new neighbours (Calgary's a friendly city!)
  • Update your driver's license & vehicle registration with Service Alberta
