💬 PRICING FAQs

Why flat-rate instead of hourly?

Hourly Calgary movers can drag out a job to inflate your bill. Flat-rate means we're motivated to work fast and well. What we quote is what you pay, period.

What about hidden fees? Fuel? Stairs?

Zero hidden fees. No fuel surcharge. No stair fees. No long-carry fees. No mattress disposal surcharge. Our quote is final.

Do you charge more for Airdrie / Cochrane / Okotoks?

Nope. Same Calgary pricing for nearby cities. We're up Hwy 2 and down Hwy 2 multiple times a week anyway.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes — 10% off for seniors (65+), students with ID, military, first responders, and repeat customers. Just mention it.

How do you take payment?

e-Transfer (preferred), cash, or credit/debit. Payment after job done or split into deposit + balance for large jobs.

Do you do quotes over text/photos?

Yes! Easiest way to quote junk pickup. Send photos of what needs to go and your address — we'll text a flat quote in under an hour.
