⚠️ WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW

  • Electrical disconnect: You'll need a licensed electrician to disconnect the 240V circuit BEFORE we arrive (~$100-200). We don't touch panels.
  • Pre-drain: Saves us 30+ min and saves you $30-50. Use a sump pump or just let it drain through a garden hose overnight.
  • Sunken/built-in tubs: Need deck demolition first. Add $300-800 depending on size.
  • Crane needed? Sometimes for tubs in fenced backyards with no access. We'll let you know during quote.
