PRIVACY POLICY

FIRE FELLAS CALGARY · LAST UPDATED 2026

Fire Fellas Calgary ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Contact info: Your name, phone number, and email when you submit a quote form or contact us
  • Service details: What service you need, address(es), date, items being moved/removed
  • Quote details: The price we quoted and details of any job
  • Usage data: Anonymous browser/device info via standard web logs (no personal tracking)

HOW WE USE IT

We use your info only to:

  • Send you a quote
  • Confirm your booking and remind you about service date
  • Communicate with you about the job (before, during, after)
  • Send invoice and request honest review (optional)
  • Improve our service quality

WHAT WE DON'T DO

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share with advertisers
  • We do not use your info for spam or unsolicited marketing
  • We do not store payment card details (handled via e-Transfer or in-person processors)

WHO SEES YOUR INFO

Your details are only seen by:

  • The Fire Fellas team members handling your quote/job
  • Insurance providers if a claim arises (extremely rare)
  • Required disclosures by Canadian law (warrant, court order)

COOKIES & TRACKING

Our website uses minimal tracking:

  • localStorage on your device to remember your form progress (e.g., quote calculator). This stays on your device only.
  • We do not currently use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking. (If we add these later, we will update this policy.)

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal info we hold on you
  • Request correction of inaccurate info
  • Request deletion of your info
  • Opt out of any communication at any time

To exercise these rights, email firefellasmoving@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy Request".

DATA RETENTION

We keep your job details for 7 years for tax and warranty purposes. Marketing communications stop the moment you ask. We delete contact info on request unless we have a legal duty to retain it.

CHILDREN

Our service is for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect info from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here and update the "Last updated" date.
