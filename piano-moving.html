🎹 WHY PIANOS NEED SPECIALISTS

A typical Calgary upright piano weighs 400-600 lbs. A baby grand is 500-700 lbs. A full grand is 900-1,200 lbs. And they're not just heavy — they're top-heavy, asymmetric, and irreplaceable.

One wrong tilt and the soundboard cracks. One careless drop on a leg and you've got $2,000+ in repairs. That's why piano moving is its own thing.

  • Remove valuables: Take down pictures/mirrors near the moving path

🧪 WHAT IF MY PIANO NEEDS TUNING AFTER?

Any piano move requires a tuning afterward — even the most careful move shifts strings slightly with vibration and temperature change. Allow 2-4 weeks for the piano to settle in the new room, then book a tuner.

Calgary piano tuners we recommend (no affiliation, just good ones our customers have used):

Tuning costs: $130-180 in Calgary.
